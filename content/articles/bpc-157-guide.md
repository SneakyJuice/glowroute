## What is BPC-157?

BPC-157 is a synthetic peptide, modeled after a protective protein found naturally in human gastric juice. The acronym stands for Body Protecting Compound-157. It has been the subject of extensive pre-clinical research for its potential to accelerate healing in various tissues, including the gut lining, tendons, ligaments, and muscles. As a peptide therapy, it is not a hormone or a steroid, but a sequence of amino acids that acts as a signaling molecule in the body to promote repair processes. It is important to note that BPC-157 is a prescription-only compound and must be administered under the supervision of a qualified healthcare provider.
