## PART 4 ā?" PROMPT TROUBLESHOOTING

### When Logo Text Renders Incorrectly
1. Break the brand name: try `"Glow Route"` (space) then recombine in Canva
2. Use fewer characters: generate icon mark only, add text in Canva/Figma
3. Ideogram is best ā?" use their web UI directly for any text-in-logo work

### When Colors Don't Match Brand
- Specify hex in prompt: `"champagne gold hex C9A96E"`
- Add: `"warm not cool, golden not yellow, ivory not pure white"`
- Avoid: asking for "cream" alone ā?" specify "ivory cream, warm off-white, not grey"

### When Results Look Too Generic
Add brand-specific differentiators:
- `"Resy meets Net-a-Porter aesthetic, for aesthetic wellness"`
- `"$500/session medspa clientele"`
- `"Italian marble lobby energy, digital"`
- Reference luxury brands: `"aesthetic quality of Tatcha, La Mer, Augustinus Bader packaging"`

### When Dark Backgrounds Look Flat
- Add: `"subtle warm ambient glow, rim light from champagne gold"`
- Add: `"deep atmospheric, not flat black, layered tonal depth"`
